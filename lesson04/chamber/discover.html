Armenia, The "Miracle City"

Armenia is a Colombian municipality, capital of the department of Quind√≠o and economic nucleus of its metropolitan area. It is one of the main cities of the Colombian coffee axis, the paisa region and the Coffee Cultural Landscape. Founded in 1889 during the Antioquian colonization, its economy was based on agriculture and thanks to the coffee boom between the 1920s and 1980s, the city experienced considerable development, from which the nickname "Miracle City" arose.
This town is located in the Golden Triangle, and is one of the main commercial and tourist centers in western Colombia.
